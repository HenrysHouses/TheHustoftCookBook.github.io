Chicken enchiladas
Kristine's Recipe, Two servings.

Serves: 2 (makes about 4 wraps)

Ingredients

  • Ingredients;
  • 1 chicken fillet
  • 500g tomato Passata / chopped tomato
  • A dash of water
  • One red and 1/2 green bell pepper
  • One medium onion
  • Couple of jalapeños slices
  • Chilli flakes
  • Hot chilli powder
  • Coriander
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cheese

Directions

Add all ingredients, except cheese, to the slow cooker and cook on high for 3 h. Remove chicken and use blender to blend the sauce until there’s no lumps. Add a few spoonfuls of sauce to the shredded chicken. Add a thin layer of sauce to a ovenproof dish. Assemble the wraps with a spoonful of sauce, then chicken with topped cheese. Roll the wraps and place in the ovenproof dish. Pour the remaining sauce over the wraps, might not need to use it all. Cover with cheese and cool on 200C for 15-20 minutes or until cheese has melted.

Oven baked chicken casserole
Kristine's Recipe, Two servings.

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken fillet
  • One small onion, chopped
  • Three garlic cloves
  • One carrot, chopped
  • 5 ish medium mushrooms, sliced
  • Half bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 1 big tbs flour
  • A dash Milk
  • 1dl Cream
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Mixed herbs
  • Chilli flakes
  • Chilli powder
  • Paprika powder
  • 1 tbs Tomato purée
  • Cheese

Directions

Cut the chicken into small bite sized pieces and fry in a pan with salt , chilli powder and paprika powder, then remove from pan. Add carrot and onion to the pan until onion is golden, add garlic, and spices until fragrant. Add the butter and flour and stir in, then add a dash of milk and stir until all lumps are gone then Add cream and tomato paste. Simmer for a little while until it’s reached right consistency, a little thick (add water if too thick). Add the chicken back into the sauce and taste if you need more spices. Transfer to an oven proof dish and spread bell pepper and mushroom on top, then add cheese. Bake on 200C for 20 minutes. Stir in the cheese and leave to rest for 5 minutes before serving.

Creamy Cheesy Chicken
Henrik's Recipe, A creamy mushroom sauce served with chicken or pork with side of your choice.

Ingredients

Sauce

4 Chicken breast / pork

1 Castello white Cheese (or another brie like cheese)

Pecorino romano (ca 50g+)

1 Packet brown mushrooms

1 Paprika

3 Cloves Garlic

1 Yellow Onion

500ml Cooking Cream

Butter

Spices

Paprika powder

Salt

Pepper

Timian

Chilli oil

Salad

Iceberg lettuce

Cherry tomatoes / regular tomatoes

Feta Cheese

Cucumber

Spinach

200-250g rice / pasta

Directions

  1. Heat the pan to medium high heat, and while you wait season the chicken with salt, pepper, timian, and paprika powder.
  2. Fry the chicken only until the outside is golden brown and take it off the pan. While its cooking start cutting the other vegetables. Start with mushrooms, onions, and garlic. The salad can be done last.
  3. As the chicken is done take it off the pan and place the cut mushrooms in the pan on medium heat. Oil is not required at this stage as we are evaporating out the water in the mushrooms.
  4. When the water in the mushrooms has evaporated and they start browning, let them get some color and add the paprika, onions, garlic and butter. We want the mushrooms to absorb it and the flavours released into the butter. Cook until the onions are soft and translucent
  5. Add the cream, castello and pecorino romano. The sauce should thicken up as it heats up and the cheese melts. If the sauce doesnt emulsify all the fat you can make a water/milk/cream and startch/flour slurry to help it.
  6. Add salt, pepper and other seasoning to taste, and finally add the chicken and its released juices into the sauce. Cover with a lid and boil on low heat for 7-10 minutes depending on the thickness.
    • If you are serving with pasta then you can use the pasta water to help with the emulsion, but be careful as the pasta water should be salted and count that into the amount of salt you add/have added into the sauce. The pasta should also be added into the sauce during the last 2 minutes before its done boiling. (subtract 2 minutes from the initial cooking time for the pasta)
  7. While waiting for the chicken to cook make the salad. Cut it as you prefer and mix with feta cheese and additional chilli oil
  8. Serve with pecorino for topping.
