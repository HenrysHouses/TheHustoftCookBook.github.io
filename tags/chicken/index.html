Chicken enchiladas
Kristine's Recipe, Two servings.

Serves: 2 (makes about 4 wraps)

Ingredients

  • Ingredients;
  • 1 chicken fillet
  • 500g tomato Passata / chopped tomato
  • A dash of water
  • One red and 1/2 green bell pepper
  • One medium onion
  • Couple of jalapeños slices
  • Chilli flakes
  • Hot chilli powder
  • Coriander
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cheese

Directions

Add all ingredients, except cheese, to the slow cooker and cook on high for 3 h. Remove chicken and use blender to blend the sauce until there’s no lumps. Add a few spoonfuls of sauce to the shredded chicken. Add a thin layer of sauce to a ovenproof dish. Assemble the wraps with a spoonful of sauce, then chicken with topped cheese. Roll the wraps and place in the ovenproof dish. Pour the remaining sauce over the wraps, might not need to use it all. Cover with cheese and cool on 200C for 15-20 minutes or until cheese has melted.

Chicken Wraps
Oven baked chicken casserole
Kristine's Recipe, Two servings.

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken fillet
  • One small onion, chopped
  • Three garlic cloves
  • One carrot, chopped
  • 5 ish medium mushrooms, sliced
  • Half bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 1 big tbs flour
  • A dash Milk
  • 1dl Cream
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Mixed herbs
  • Chilli flakes
  • Chilli powder
  • Paprika powder
  • 1 tbs Tomato purée
  • Cheese

Directions

Cut the chicken into small bite sized pieces and fry in a pan with salt , chilli powder and paprika powder, then remove from pan. Add carrot and onion to the pan until onion is golden, add garlic, and spices until fragrant. Add the butter and flour and stir in, then add a dash of milk and stir until all lumps are gone then Add cream and tomato paste. Simmer for a little while until it’s reached right consistency, a little thick (add water if too thick). Add the chicken back into the sauce and taste if you need more spices. Transfer to an oven proof dish and spread bell pepper and mushroom on top, then add cheese. Bake on 200C for 20 minutes. Stir in the cheese and leave to rest for 5 minutes before serving.

Chicken Curry
Henrik's Recipe, An indian inspired chicken curry

Ingredients

  • 650 grams Chicken
  • Rice
  • two cartons of coconut milk
  • Broccoli
  • 3-4 Carrots
  • A thumb of ginger
  • half an onion
  • 2-3 gloves of garlic
  • Curry seasoning
  • Garam Masala seasoning
  • Paprika powder
  • Salt
  • 1 tbsp Sugar
  • Pepper

Directions

Chicken:

  1. Season the chicken breasts or cut it bite sized pieces and then season. Cooking oil can be helpful to cover the chicken in the seasoning when it is cut into pieces.
  2. Preheat a frying pan to high heat.
  3. Fry the chicken in the pan until it develops a brown color then remove from the pan.
  4. Start on the sauce and put on the rice to cook.
  5. Once the sauce is ready let the chicken rest in the boiling sauce for 8-10 min.
