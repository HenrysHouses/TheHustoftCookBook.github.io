Chicken enchiladas
Kristine's Recipe, Two servings. Serves: 2 (makes about 4 wraps) Ingredients Ingredients; 1 chicken fillet 500g tomato Passata / chopped tomato A dash of water One red and 1/2 green bell pepper One medium onion Couple of jalapeños slices Chilli flakes Hot chilli powder Coriander Salt Pepper Cheese Directions Add all ingredients, except cheese, to the slow cooker and cook on high for 3 h. Remove chicken and use blender to blend the sauce until there’s no lumps.

Oven baked chicken casserole
Kristine's Recipe, Two servings. Ingredients 1 chicken fillet One small onion, chopped Three garlic cloves One carrot, chopped 5 ish medium mushrooms, sliced Half bell pepper, chopped 1 tbs butter 1 big tbs flour A dash Milk 1dl Cream Salt Pepper Mixed herbs Chilli flakes Chilli powder Paprika powder 1 tbs Tomato purée Cheese Directions Cut the chicken into small bite sized pieces and fry in a pan with salt , chilli powder and paprika powder, then remove from pan.

Chicken Curry
Henrik's Recipe, An indian inspired chicken curry Ingredients 650 grams Chicken Rice two cartons of coconut milk Broccoli 3-4 Carrots A thumb of ginger half an onion 2-3 gloves of garlic Curry seasoning Garam Masala seasoning Paprika powder Salt 1 tbsp Sugar Pepper Directions Chicken: Season the chicken breasts or cut it bite sized pieces and then season. Cooking oil can be helpful to cover the chicken in the seasoning when it is cut into pieces. Preheat a frying pan to high heat.
