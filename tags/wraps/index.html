Chicken enchiladas
Kristine's Recipe, Two servings. Serves: 2 (makes about 4 wraps) Ingredients Ingredients; 1 chicken fillet 500g tomato Passata / chopped tomato A dash of water One red and 1/2 green bell pepper One medium onion Couple of jalapeños slices Chilli flakes Hot chilli powder Coriander Salt Pepper Cheese Directions Add all ingredients, except cheese, to the slow cooker and cook on high for 3 h. Remove chicken and use blender to blend the sauce until there’s no lumps.
