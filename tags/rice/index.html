Chicken Curry
Henrik's Recipe, An indian inspired chicken curry Ingredients 650 grams Chicken Rice two cartons of coconut milk Broccoli 3-4 Carrots A thumb of ginger half an onion 2-3 gloves of garlic Curry seasoning Garam Masala seasoning Paprika powder Salt 1 tbsp Sugar Pepper Directions Chicken: Season the chicken breasts or cut it bite sized pieces and then season. Cooking oil can be helpful to cover the chicken in the seasoning when it is cut into pieces. Preheat a frying pan to high heat.
