Chicken Curry Rice

Ingredients

  • 650 grams Chicken
  • Rice
  • two cartons of coconut milk
  • Broccoli
  • 3-4 Carrots
  • A thumb of ginger
  • half an onion
  • 2-3 gloves of garlic
  • Curry seasoning
  • Garam Masala seasoning
  • Paprika powder
  • Salt
  • 1 tbsp Sugar
  • Pepper

Directions

Chicken:

  1. Season the chicken breasts or cut it bite sized pieces and then season. Cooking oil can be helpful to cover the chicken in the seasoning when it is cut into pieces.
  2. Preheat a frying pan to high heat.
  3. Fry the chicken in the pan until it develops a brown color then remove from the pan.
  4. Start on the sauce and put on the rice to cook.
  5. Once the sauce is ready let the chicken rest in the boiling sauce for 8-10 min.

Sauce:

  1. Peel and chop the carrot in to slices.
  2. Start cooking them in the same pan and drizzle 1 tbsp sugar on top.
  3. Peel then Grate the ginger and add to the pan.
  4. Cut and add to the pan in order
    1. Broccoli
    2. Onion
    3. Garlic
  5. Once the Onions are done pour in the coconut milk.
  6. Season the sauce with the curry seasoning and garam masala until it gains a bright yellow color.
  7. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Add the chicken and its juices into the sauce and cover with a lid.
  9. Let the chicken cook for 8-10 min and then serve with the rice.
