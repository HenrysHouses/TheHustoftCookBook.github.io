Honey-Chilli Mustard Chicken

Chicken Honey Chilli Couscous

Honey-Chilli Mustard Chicken with Greek Roasted Vegetables & Couscous

Ingredients (serves 2)

Chicken & marinade

•    2 chicken legs (bone-in, skin on)
•    1–2 tsp tomato purée
•    Salt & pepper
•    Paprika, garlic powder, mixed herbs
•    Two garlic cloves
•    olive oil drizzle
•    A dash of apple cider vinegar / lemon juice

Honey chilli mustard glaze

•    1 tbsp honey
•    1 tbsp wholegrain or Dijon mustard
•    2 tbs sweet chilli sauce

Vegetables

•    1 zucchini, chopped into half moons
•    1-1.5 red onions, cut into wedges
•    1-1.5 red peppers, chopped
•    Handful cherry tomatoes
•    Olive oil
•    Salt, pepper, dried oregano, garlic powder 
•    A dash of Apple cider vinegar

Couscous

•    125g couscous
•    125ml chicken stock or one stock cube + water

Extras

•    Garlic bread (optional, to serve)

Method

Roast chicken & vegetables

Preheat oven to 200°C fan (220°C conventional). Place chicken and vegetables on a roasting tray. Rub chicken with all ingredients listed. Place chicken skin-side up on the tray. Roast chicken for 10 minutes. Season vegetables with all ingredients listed. After the initial 10 min for the chicken, add all vegetables to the tray and roast for another 20 min.

Add glaze

Mix all glaze ingredients. Brush over chicken. Return to oven for 10until sticky and caramelised. Chicken total cook time: 40 minutes. (No need to turn the chicken. Stir vegetables once if needed.)

Garlic bread (optional)

Bake for 10 minutes (usually at same oven temp).

⸻

Couscous

Put couscous in a bowl. Pour over 125ml boiling chicken stock/ chicken stock cube and boiling water. Cover and leave for 5–10 minutes. Fluff with a fork.

⸻

Finish

Mix roasted vegetables and tray juices into couscous. Serve with glazed chicken and garlic bread on the side. Optional: squeeze of lemon or fresh herbs to finish.
